How to Choose the Right Dumpster for Your Project

When choosing the right dumpster, the process can seem overwhelming, especially if you’ve never rented one. Here’s what you need to consider when selecting the best dumpster for your needs:

1. Know Your Project Size

The size of your dumpster will depend on the scale of your project. At Javis Dumpster Rental Orlando, we offer dumpsters in several sizes:

  • 10-yard dumpster: Ideal for small cleanouts, garage cleanups, or minor renovations.
  • 20-yard dumpster: Perfect for medium-sized projects like remodeling a kitchen or cleaning a basement.
  • 30-yard dumpster: An excellent choice for extensive home renovations or small commercial construction projects.
  • 40-yard dumpster: Best for large commercial projects, construction sites, or major cleanouts.

2. Consider the Type of Waste

Different types of waste may require special disposal. For example, hazardous materials like chemicals or electronics may need to be disposed of separately. When renting a dumpster, be sure to clarify what types of materials you’re disposing of to ensure everything is handled correctly.

3. Estimate How Long You’ll Need It

Think about how long you’ll need the dumpster. Some projects may take only a few days, while others require a week or more. Javis Dumpster Rental Orlando offers flexible rental periods, so you can keep the dumpster as long as you need.

4. Choose the Right Location

Before the dumpster is delivered, make sure you have a safe and accessible location for it. The area should be large enough for the dumpster and easy for the delivery truck to access. Our team can help you choose the best spot if you’re unsure.
